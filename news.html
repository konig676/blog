18th May, 2021 IT

Once a supercomputer, now a processor. Google unveils new chip

On May 18, 2021, Google announced the fourth-generation Tensor Processing Unit (TPU). The new solutions, according to the developers, are twice as fast as the previous model. Introducing the new Tensor Processing Unit, Google CEO Sundar Pichai noted that these chips can be combined into blocks consisting of 4096 TPUs. The performance of one such unit can exceed 1 exaflops and correspond to the capacity of 10 million laptops with the most advanced technical characteristics.
